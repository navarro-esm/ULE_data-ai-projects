# Lecture Slides – Research Projects in Data Engineering and Artificial Intelligence

This repository contains the official lecture slides for the course **Research Projects in Data Engineering and Artificial Intelligence**, taught at the **Universidad de León**.

The materials cover key topics discussed throughout the semester, including:

- Project design and planning
- Data workflows and pipelines
- Artificial Intelligence methodologies
- Collaborative research strategies

Each presentation is organized by session and reflects the structure and content delivered during lectures.
